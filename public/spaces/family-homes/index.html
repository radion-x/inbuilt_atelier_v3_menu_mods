Spaces We Work In

Joinery for
Family Homes

Family homes demand joinery that is both beautiful and genuinely functional. We design with real family life in mind — abundant storage, durable finishes, and clever configurations that keep every room organised without looking clinical.

Built for Life

Storage That Actually Works for Your Family

The challenge in a family home is not finding space for more stuff — it is designing storage so that every member of the family knows where things go and can access them independently. Our designs place function first and layer beauty over the top.

We design wardrobes for adults and children differently, configure home office joinery around the way parents actually work, and approach laundry and mudroom cabinetry as high-use, practical spaces first.

What We Deliver

Joinery Crafted for This Space

Children’s Wardrobes

Adjustable interiors that grow with your children — lower hanging for small items now, easily reconfigured for full-length hanging as they get older.

Master Bedroom Wardrobes

Generous storage for couples: double hanging, long hanging, shoe storage, drawers, and dedicated accessories sections — everything has its place.

Mudroom & Entry Joinery

Coat hooks, shoe storage, bag cubbies, and bench seating at the entry — the joinery that absorbs the chaos before it enters the rest of the home.

Laundry Cabinetry

Overhead cabinets, under-sink storage, pull-out hampers, and folding bench surfaces — laundry joinery that makes the least favourite room feel resolved.

Pantry Fitout

Full pantry fitout with adjustable shelving, pull-out drawers, spice racks, and a prep bench insert — designed around how your family actually shops and cooks.

Home Office Built-Ins

Built-in desks, reference shelving, filing drawers, and printer cabinetry — so working from home does not mean the study looks like a home office from IKEA.
