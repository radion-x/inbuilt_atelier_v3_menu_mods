Whole-Home Solutions

Joinery &
Cabinetry

Every room in a home has storage needs. We design and build fitted cabinetry that solves them beautifully — from entryway joinery to full-height library walls and everything in between.

What We Build

Cabinetry for Every Room

Whether it is a single storage challenge or a whole-home joinery programme, we approach every project with the same attention and craft.

Library & Bookcase Walls

Floor-to-ceiling shelving with ladder rails, integrated display niches, and concealed cabinet doors — the centrepiece of any living or study space.

Hallway & Entry Storage

Full-height entry cabinetry with shoe storage, coat hooks, key drawers, and display ledges — hard-working but refined.

Laundry Cabinetry

Custom cabinetry to make every laundry work efficiently — appliance housing, bench tops, hanging rails, and linen storage.

Pantry & Butler’s Pantry

Pull-out drawers, spice racks, appliance garages, and open shelving configured for how your household actually operates.

Under-Stair Cabinetry

We make use of every awkward volume — angled drawers, wine storage, pull-out units, and concealed doors that look intentional.

Bespoke Display & Niche Work

Floating shelves, art niches, fireplace surrounds, and custom joinery details that give a room its defining character.

Our Approach

Joinery That Integrates, Not Just Decorates

The best joinery disappears into the architecture. We design with your existing cornices, skirtings, ceiling heights, and floor finishes in mind — so fitted cabinetry looks as if it was always there.

We handle the complete process from design through to installation, and we never subcontract fitment. The craftspeople who build your cabinetry are the same people who install it.
