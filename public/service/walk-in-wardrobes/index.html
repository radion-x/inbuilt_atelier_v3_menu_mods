A walk-in wardrobe is not just storage — it is a room in its own right. We design dressing rooms that are as considered and beautiful as the rest of your home.

A Room to Dress In

Your Wardrobe, Elevated to a Sanctuary

We treat walk-in wardrobes as living spaces. Every material, every proportion, and every detail is chosen to create an environment that feels calm, ordered, and beautifully personal.

From compact en-suite dressing rooms to grand walk-through robes with island benching and full-length mirrors, we design layouts that suit your wardrobe size, lifestyle, and the architecture of your home.

The Details

Island Benches, Display Shelving & More

A freestanding island unit with velvet-lined jewellery drawers, fold-out mirror, and marble or stone top transforms your robe into something truly special.

Open shelving with back-lit display niches for handbags, shoes, and accessories — designed with the depth and spacing your collection requires. Framed or frameless mirrors positioned to maximise light.

Specifications

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don’t

Double & Triple Hanging

Configurable rails at multiple heights for dresses, suits, folded shirts, and short garments — maximising capacity without clutter.

Shoe Storage Systems

Angled display shelves, pull-out shoe drawers, and enclosed cubbies — built to the exact dimensions of your collection.

Integrated Lighting

LED strip under shelves, puck lights in display niches, and warm-white overhead pendants or downlights wired before install.

Premium Finishes

Matte lacquer, timber veneer, linen-textured board, and brushed brass or matte black hardware — all selected at our showroom with samples.

Pass-Through Designs

Walk-through robes connecting bedroom to ensuite — a design detail that elevates how a home feels and flows.

10-Year Warranty
