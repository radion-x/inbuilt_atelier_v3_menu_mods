Our Philosophy

The Atelier
Difference

An atelier is a workshop where craft is taken seriously. At Inbuilt Atelier, that means we approach every wardrobe and joinery commission with the same rigour a European craftsman applies to bespoke furniture. No catalogue configurations. No compromise on materials.

We work from our Double Bay studio with a small, dedicated team — designers who draw every layout by hand first, and installers who treat your home with the care it deserves. The result is joinery that feels like it was always part of the architecture.

Our Story
How We Work

From Brief to Beautiful

Every Inbuilt Atelier commission follows a clear, considered process — so you know exactly what to expect at every stage.

01

Consult

We visit your space or meet at our Double Bay showroom. We listen, measure, and understand how you want to use the space before we draw a single line.

02

Design

Our designers produce detailed drawings and 3D renders. You review, refine, and approve every dimension, finish, and hardware detail before anything is made.

03

Craft

Your joinery is built in our workshop using premium materials and European hardware. Precision-cut panels, hand-fitted joints, and quality-checked at every stage.

04

Install

Our own installation team fits your joinery with meticulous care. We protect your surfaces, work cleanly, and leave your home in perfect order — only the wardrobe remains.
